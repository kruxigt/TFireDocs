TimeSeriesDataLink (Developer)

The Time Series Data Link, or short TSDLink, is a link between an entry and a time series (typically residing in the SimpleFastDB). The TSDLink is a subtype of AssetDataLink in a way that another subtype of AssetDataLink could possibly replace it.

Technicalities

In practical terms the TSDLink is the uppermost condensate of an entry. It links

. It will always be the uppermost condensate of an entry and is a subtype of AssetDataLink.

  • timeseriesrange is a vector of unit ranges where each range is used together with the index_mod

of the time series data link together with that of the time series to calculate the range of values in the linked time series that are used for the sample.

  • minimumval and maximumval are the minimum and maximum value in timeseriesrange. They are used

for calculating the data range for nonrepainting layers.

  • index_mod is copied from the time series in the database at the time of creation of the data link.

This is to be able to link to the correct place in the time series even after the time series has been updated with new data.
